# 🏷️ 01 — Brand Fundamentals & Strategy

> 🟢 Beginner | Prereq: — | ~4 min

Brand = **gut feeling** people have about you. Not what YOU say — what THEY say. (Marty Neumeier)

---

## 1.1 Brand vs Product vs Commodity

```
Commodity → Product → BRAND
(price only)  (features)  (meaning + premium)
```

| Stage | Differentiator | Price |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| Commodity | None | Market rate |
| Product | Features, quality | Small margin |
| **Brand** | **Meaning, emotion, trust** | **Significant premium** |

---

## 1.2 Core Strategy Components

| Component | Question | Example (Nike) |
|-----------|----------|----------------|
| **Purpose** | Why exist beyond money? | "Bring inspiration to every athlete" |
| **Vision** | Future state? | "A world where..." |
| **Mission** | How to get there? | "We will [action] for [audience] by [method]" |
| **Values** | Non-negotiable principles? | Tested in crisis — not posters |

---

## 1.3 Positioning Statement

```
For [audience] who [need], [brand] is the [category] that [key benefit] because [reason].
```

### Positioning Map — Find White Space
```
HIGH PRICE × LOW EMOTIONAL = Luxury  |  HIGH PRICE × HIGH EMOTIONAL = Premium
LOW PRICE × LOW EMOTIONAL = Budget   |  LOW PRICE × HIGH EMOTIONAL = Value
```
→ Occupy the quadrant no competitor owns.

---

## 1.4 Brand Architecture

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Branded House** (Monolithic) | Master brand | Apple, FedEx |
| **Endorsed** | Sub-brands + parent | Courtyard by Marriott |
| **House of Brands** (Pluralistic) | Independent brands | P&G (Tide, Pampers, Gillette) |

---

## 1.5 Building Process

```
RESEARCH(2-4w) → STRATEGY(2-6w) → IDENTITY(4-12w) → TOUCHPOINTS(ongoing) → LAUNCH(1-4w) → MANAGE(continuous)
```
