# 🏷️ 07 — Brand Evolution & Competitive Positioning

> 🔴 Advanced | Prereq: 01, 05 | ~4 min

Brands aren't static. When to evolve vs revolutionize, competitive positioning, rebranding process.

---

## 7.1 Brand Lifecycle

```
Launch → Growth → Maturity → Saturation → EVOLVE or DECLINE
```

| Phase | Focus | Risk |
|-------|-------|------|
| Launch | Awareness, differentiation | Being forgotten |
| Growth | Scale touchpoints, build equity | Inconsistency at scale |
| Maturity | Defend position, optimize | Complacency |
| Evolution | Refresh without losing equity | Alienating customers |
| Reinvention | Fundamental change | Losing recognition |

---

## 7.2 Evolution vs Revolution

| | Evolution (Refresh) | Revolution (Rebrand) |
|---|---------------------|----------------------|
| Scope | Visual refinement, tone adjustment | New name, logo, positioning |
| Risk | Low | High — may lose recognition |
| Timeline | 3-6 months | 12-24 months |
| Trigger | Brand feels dated | Strategy fundamentally changed, merger, crisis |

```
Evolve: logo dated but strategy sound, competitors modernized, need digital refresh
Rebrand: strategy changed, name no longer fits, merger, severe negative associations
```

---

## 7.3 Competitive Positioning

| Strategy | Focus | Example |
|----------|-------|---------|
| **Cost Leadership** | Lowest price | Walmart, Ryanair |
| **Differentiation** | Unique value | Apple, BMW |
| **Focus/Niche** | Specific segment | Patagonia, Harley-Davidson |

### Brand Equation
```
BRAND = FUNCTIONAL + EMOTIONAL + SELF-EXPRESSION BENEFIT
Volvo = Safety + Peace of mind + "I'm a responsible parent"
Nike  = Performance + Inspiration + "I'm an athlete"
Apple = Technology + Creativity + "I think different"
```

> Deeper benefit level = harder to copy. Anyone can make a safe car. No one copies Volvo's ownership of "safety" in the mind.

---

## 7.4 Rebranding Process

```
DISCOVERY(4-6w) → STRATEGY(4-8w) → IDENTITY(8-16w) → ASSETS(4-8w) → LAUNCH(2-4w) → MONITOR(ongoing)
```

### Launch: Internal First, External Second
```
INTERNAL: Town hall → training → updated assets → employee merch
EXTERNAL: Coordinated launch → press kit → social → website → email customers → redirect old assets
```
> ❌ Never launch Friday. ❌ Don't surprise. ✅ Own the narrative. ✅ Explain WHY.

---

## 7.5 Brand Extensions

| Strategy | Risk | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| Line extension | Low | Coke → Diet Coke |
| Category extension | Medium | Apple → Watch |
| Brand stretch | **High** | Harley-Davidson → perfume (FAILED) |

### Extension Test
```
Leverage existing equity? → Enhance parent brand? → Logical fit? → Credibility in new category?
```
> Failed extensions damage the PARENT brand. Vet ruthlessly.

---

## 7.6 Brand Health Dashboard

| KPI | Target | Frequency |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Unaided recall | Top 3 in category | Quarterly |
| NPS | >50 | Monthly |
| Sentiment | >80% positive | Monthly |
| Share of voice | ≥ market share | Monthly |
| Touchpoint consistency | >90% | Quarterly |

> Red flags: declining share of voice or consistency → fix before awareness drops.
