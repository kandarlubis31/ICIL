# 🎨 02 — Color Harmony & Schemes

> 🟢 Beginner | Prereq: 01 | ~4 min

Harmony = visually pleasing color combinations. Creates order, supports hierarchy, builds mood. Like musical harmony — right notes please, clashing notes hurt.

---

## 2.1 Seven Harmony Schemes

| # | Scheme | Colors | Wheel Position | Vibe | Best For | Key Rule |
|---|--------|--------|---------------|------|----------|----------|
| 1 | **Monochromatic** | 1 hue × variations | — | Elegant, clean | Minimalist, dashboards | Ensure enough contrast between variations |
| 2 | **Complementary** | 2 | Opposite 180° | Bold, high contrast | CTAs, posters, sports | NOT 50:50 — one dominant, one accent |
| 3 | **Analogous** | 3 | Adjacent | Calm, natural | Nature themes, gradients | One dominant, one support, one accent |
| 4 | **Triadic** | 3 | 120° apart | Playful, vibrant | Games, kids, fun brands | Let 1 dominate, 2 as accents |
| 5 | **Split-Complementary** | 3 | 1 base + 2 beside complement | Safer contrast | UI needing pop without aggression | Base dominant, 2 balanced accents |
| 6 | **Tetradic** | 4 | 2 complementary pairs | Rich, complex | Colorful branding | One dominant, 3 subordinate |
| 7 | **Square** | 4 | 90° apart | Balanced, bold | Complex illustrations | Same as tetradic |

| Extra | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Achromatic** | Only B/W/Gray (+ optional 1 accent = "Achromatic + Accent") |

---

## 2.2 The 60-30-10 Rule

| % | Role | Example |
|---|------|---------|
| **60%** | Dominant / Background | White, light gray, brand primary |
| **30%** | Secondary | Cards, sidebars, secondary sections |
| **10%** | Accent | CTAs, icons, highlights, links |

---

## 2.3 Scheme Selection

| Need | Pick |
|------|------|
| Clean & minimal | Monochromatic |
| Pop & contrast | Complementary (careful!) |
| Calm & natural | Analogous |
| Playful & balanced | Triadic |
| Safe contrast | Split-Complementary |
| Rich & complex | Tetradic / Square |

### Golden Rules
1. Never use pure colors — always tweak saturation/value
2. One color must dominate — avoid equal distribution
3. Test in grayscale — hierarchy should still be clear
4. Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for text

---

## ⚡ Action Checklist
- [ ] Pick one scheme based on brand personality (not just aesthetics)
- [ ] Apply 60-30-10 distribution before tweaking individual colors
- [ ] Test all color combinations in grayscale mode
- [ ] Verify text contrast meets WCAG AA (4.5:1) for your accent color
- [ ] For complementary schemes: desaturate slightly to avoid eye vibration
