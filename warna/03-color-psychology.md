# 🎨 03 — Color Psychology

> 🟡 Intermediate | Prereq: 01 | ~5 min

Color influences emotions, behavior, and decisions — 93% of buyers focus on visuals, brand recognition jumps 80% with color. Response happens within 90 seconds. But effects are **probabilistic**, not deterministic — context, culture, and combination matter.

---

## 3.1 Warm Colors 🔥

| Color | Emotion | Physical Effect | Brands | Best For | ⚠️ Caution |
|-------|---------|----------------|--------|----------|-----------|
| 🔴 **Red** | Passion, urgency, danger, power | ↑ heart rate, appetite, adrenaline | Coca-Cola, Netflix, YouTube | CTAs, sales, food, errors | Anxiety if overused; eye fatigue |
| 🟠 **Orange** | Enthusiasm, creativity, warmth | Gentle stimulation, friendly | Fanta, Amazon, Home Depot | CTAs, subscribe, creative | Can look cheap if wrong tone |
| 🟡 **Yellow** | Optimism, happiness, clarity | Most eye-catching; mental stimulation | McDonald's, IKEA, Snapchat | Attention grabbers, kids, food | Eye fatigue; babies cry more in yellow rooms |

---

## 3.2 Cool Colors ❄️

| Color | Emotion | Physical Effect | Brands | Best For | ⚠️ Caution |
|-------|---------|----------------|--------|----------|-----------|
| 🟢 **Green** | Balance, growth, harmony | Most restful on eyes (no adjustment) | Spotify, Starbucks, Whole Foods | Environment, health, finance | "Money" or "envy" association |
| 🔵 **Blue** | Trust, security, professionalism | ↓ heart rate, calming, focus | Facebook, LinkedIn, PayPal, IBM | Corporate, tech, healthcare | Can feel cold; suppresses appetite |
| 🟣 **Purple** | Luxury, mystery, creativity | Calming + stimulating (unique) | Twitch, Yahoo, Cadbury | Luxury, beauty, gaming | Can feel "not serious" |

---

## 3.3 Neutrals ⚪⚫

| Color | Emotion | Brands | Best For |
|-------|---------|--------|----------|
| ⚪ **White** | Clean, pure, simple, open | Apple, Nike, Google | Background, minimal, healthcare |
| ⚫ **Black** | Power, elegance, sophistication | Chanel, Sony, Uber | Luxury, premium, typography |
| 🩶 **Gray** | Neutral, professional, calm | — | Subtle backgrounds, secondary text |
| 🤎 **Brown** | Reliable, warm, earthy, honest | UPS, Hershey's | Natural, organic, rustic |
| 🩷 **Pink** | Feminine, soft, love (hot = energetic; pastel = calming) | Barbie, Baskin Robbins | Beauty, romance, feminine products |

---

## 3.4 Color × Conversion

| Experiment | Result |
|------------|--------|
| Performable: Red vs Green CTA | **Red +21%** |
| HubSpot: Red vs Green CTA | **Red +21%** |
| RIPT: Green vs Red CTA | **Red +6.3%** |
| Beamax: Blue vs Red vs Green | **Red +50%+** |

> Red wins CTAs often — but **context-dependent**. If page is blue, orange may stand out more.

## 3.5 Color × Industry Matrix

| Industry | Colors | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| Finance / Banking | Blue, Green | Trust, stability |
| Food / Restaurant | Red, Yellow, Orange | Appetite, warmth |
| Healthcare | Blue, White, Green | Clean, calm, trust |
| Tech / Startup | Blue, Purple, Black | Innovation, trust |
| Luxury | Black, Gold, Dark Purple | Elegance, exclusivity |
| Eco / Green | Green, Brown, Cream | Nature, earth |
| Beauty / Cosmetic | Pink, Purple, Black, White | Feminine, luxury |
| Children | All bright colors | Playful, fun |

---

## ⚡ Action Checklist
- [ ] Map brand personality → color emotion before picking palette
- [ ] Test primary CTA color with A/B (red often wins, but test your context)
- [ ] Verify color-industry alignment: finance ≠ red, food ≠ blue (appetite killer)
- [ ] Check cultural color meanings for target markets (white = purity in West, mourning in East)
- [ ] Use color psychology as guide, not absolute — always validate with real users
