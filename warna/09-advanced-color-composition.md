# 🎨 09 — Advanced Color Composition

> 🔴 Advanced | Prereq: 02, 03, 05 | ~5 min

Capstone: cinematic color grading, atmospheric perspective, color scripts, luxury formulas.

---

## 9.1 Color Grading Pipeline

```
ORIGINAL → CORRECTION (white balance, exposure, contrast)
         → GRADING (shadows X, midtones Y, highlights Z, tint, vignette)
```

**Classic split-tone**: Highlights = Warm (orange/gold) + Shadows = Cool (blue/teal).
→ **Teal & Orange** — Hollywood standard. Skin = orange complementary to teal → subject pops.

---

## 9.2 Atmospheric Perspective

Distant objects naturally appear: more blue, lower contrast, reduced saturation, brighter.

| Layer | Treatment |
|-------|-----------|
| **Foreground** | Full contrast, full saturation, crisp detail |
| **Midground** | Medium contrast, saturation dropping |
| **Background** | Low contrast, low saturation, blue haze |
| **Far BG** | Nearly flat, sky/horizon color |

→ Creates depth without 3D.

---

## 9.3 Color Scripts (Storytelling)

Pixar/Disney technique — palette sequence that tells story:

| Act | Palette | Emotion |
|-----|---------|---------|
| **Setup** | Bright, saturated, warm | Normal world |
| **Conflict** | Darken, desaturate | Problems emerge |
| **Climax** | Extreme contrast, bold | Peak conflict |
| **Resolution** | Bright returns, richer | Resolution |

### UI/UX Color Script:
```
ONBOARDING  → Cheerful, warm, pastel
DASHBOARD   → Clean, neutral + blue accent
TASK/ACTION → Energy, higher saturation
ERROR/ISSUE → Red, high contrast
SUCCESS     → Green, bright, satisfying
```

---

## 9.4 Visual Hierarchy via Color

| Level | Role | Treatment |
|-------|------|-----------|
| 1 | **Hero / Brand** | Most striking, highest saturation, limited area |
| 2 | **Interactive** | Distinct color, clearly clickable |
| 3 | **Supporting** | Muted, differentiate sections |
| 4 | **Background** | Most neutral, low contrast between surfaces |

---

## 9.5 Temperature Balance (80-20 Rule)

| Mix | Effect |
|-----|--------|
| 100% warm | Boiling, aggressive, exhausting |
| 100% cool | Frozen, sterile, uninviting |
| **80% cool + 20% warm** | Calm but alive ✅ |
| **80% warm + 20% cool** | Warm but not overwhelming ✅ |

---

## 9.6 Luxury Color Formulas

| Style | Colors | Ratio |
|-------|--------|-------|
| **Luxury Dark** | Black + Gold(#D4AF37) + Cream | 70% / 20% / 10% |
| **Luxury Light** | White + Rose Gold + Black | 70% / 20% / 10% |
| **Modern Premium** | Dark Navy + White + Electric Blue | 50% / 40% / 10% |
