# 🎨 04 — Color in Culture

> 🟡 Intermediate | Prereq: 03 | ~5 min

Same color = opposite meanings across cultures. Global design MUST account for this. Wrong color = miscommunication or offense.

---

## 4.1 Color × Culture Matrix

| Color | 🇺🇸 West | 🇨🇳 China | 🇯🇵 Japan | 🇮🇳 India | 🌍 Mid-East | ⚠️ Watch For |
|-------|---------|----------|----------|----------|------------|-------------|
| 🔴 **Red** | Passion, danger, STOP | **Good fortune**, happiness, weddings | Life, sun, anger | Purity, weddings | — | China: DON'T use for names (taboo) |
| ⚪ **White** | Purity, weddings, clean | **Death**, mourning, bad luck | Death, mourning | Grief (widows) | Purity, peace | East Asia: DON'T for wedding/healthcare |
| ⚫ **Black** | Elegance, mourning | Honor, power (NOT mourning) | Mystery, destruction | Evil, misfortune | Grief, rebirth | — |
| 🟡 **Yellow** | Happiness BUT cowardice | **Imperial**, honor, royal | Courage, nobility | Purity, knowledge | — | Egypt: mourning; Germany: jealousy |
| 🟢 **Green** | Nature, luck, GO | Clean BUT green hat = **wife cheating** | Life, freshness | — | Sacred Islamic color | China/Indonesia: negative connotations |
| 🔵 **Blue** | Trust, authority, calm | Eternity, immortality | — | — | — | Iran/Korea: mourning |
| 🟣 **Purple** | Royal, luxury, creativity | Immortality, nobility | Wealth, aristocracy | — | — | Thailand/Brazil: mourning |

---

## 4.3 Design Rules for Global Audiences

| ✅ DO | ❌ DON'T |
|-------|---------|
| Research target market | Assume universality |
| Use blue as anchor (safest globally) | Use white for wedding/healthcare in East Asia |
| Test with native users | Use green in Indonesia/China without context |
| Consider dual palettes per region | Assume red = danger everywhere (China = fortune!) |
| Use neutral secondary/tertiary | Mix sacred colors casually |

### Safety Ranking
```
SAFEST:   Blue > Green (non-hat context) > White (clean context)
RISKIEST: Purple (mourning), Yellow (mourning + envy + cowardice), Green (hat/death/forbidden)
```
