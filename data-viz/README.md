# ЁЯУК Faculty of Data Visualization тАФ Overview

> **7 Courses** | Level: Beginner тЖТ Advanced | Domain: Data Visualization & Visual Communication

Data visualization is the art and science of encoding quantitative information into visual form. It sits at the intersection of statistics, perception, design, and storytelling. This faculty covers the complete spectrum: from the grammar of graphics and visual encoding theory through chart selection, color science for data, dashboard design, interactive dynamics, narrative techniques, and accessibility. When an AI agent needs to design charts, build dashboards, or tell data stories, this is the faculty to load.
